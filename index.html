Akira Toriyama Artist receive their inspiration from what is around them, and AK had his fair share of works that inspired his journey to becoming a manga artist and further beyond. While Journey to the West inspired the story of Dragon Ball, Akira Toriyama was inspired by Jackie Chan's films throughout the series.
